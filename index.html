Who We Are

About Light Optical

Our Heritage

Founded in 1968, Light Optical has spent decades perfecting the art of vision care. In 1991, we moved to a new, representative building on Jl. Lengkong Besar — establishing ourselves as the largest and grandest optical store in Bandung.

100% Original Guarantee

We provide the most complete collection of exclusive global eyewear brands, all backed by our 100% Original warranty. Every frame, lens, and accessory is sourced through authorized channels.

Something for Everyone

Our collections follow the latest trends for all ages — men, women, teenagers, children, and seniors. From frames and sunglasses to lenses, contact lenses, solutions, and eyewear accessories.

Customer-First Approach

Your vision is personal, and so is our service. Our experienced team takes the time to understand your lifestyle, prescription, and style preferences to recommend the perfect eyewear solution.

Precision Lenses for Every Lifestyle

We fit your frames with lenses from world-renowned manufacturers, tailored to your prescription and daily needs.

Single Vision

One prescription power across the entire lens — ideal for nearsightedness, farsightedness, and astigmatism correction.

Progressive

Seamless multifocal lenses that let you see clearly at distance, intermediate, and near without visible lines.

Blue Light Blocking

Designed to filter high-energy blue light from screens, helping reduce digital eye strain during long hours of use.

Transitions

Light-responsive lenses that darken outdoors and remain clear indoors, giving you comfort and UV protection in one.
